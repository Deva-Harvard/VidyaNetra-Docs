Setup
Enter exam details

Upload
Upload the question paper — AI extracts the blueprint
Upload a PDF or image of the question paper. VidyaNetra's AI automatically extracts every question, its marks, answer type, and choice rules — ready for teacher confirmation.
🤖 Auto-extracts all questions & marks
🔀 Detects choice rules (answer any 5 of 6)

Upload
Upload the rubric — AI maps scoring rules
Upload the answer key or rubric. VidyaNetra extracts model answers and per-step scoring rules, mapping them to each question in the blueprint.
🗝️ Model answers extracted per question
📊 Per-step marks mapped automatically
✅ Teacher reviews before grading starts

Students
Upload student answer sheets
Upload answer sheets one per student or in bulk. VidyaNetra runs OCR, evaluates, and shows live progress in the class roster — marks appear as each sheet is processed.
👥 5 students · OCR → Eval → Done
⚡ Live progress per student
📥 Bulk or student-by-student upload

Review
AI evaluation — question by question
For each question, VidyaNetra shows the student's extracted answer, AI analysis per sub-part, marks awarded, and confidence level. Teachers can override any mark before finalising.
🧠 Sub-part level AI analysis
💬 Reasoning shown for every mark
✏️ Teacher override with one click
🎯 HIGH CONFIDENCE flag on reliable evaluations

Analytics
Instant class analytics overview
Once evaluations are accepted, Netra Analytics computes class-wide statistics instantly — average, median, highest, lowest, fail count, and missed criteria — no manual calculation needed.

Analytics · Students
Performance heatmap & per-student drill-down
The Students tab shows a question-wise performance heatmap for the whole class, score distribution bands, and question difficulty ranking. Click any student to see their full question-by-question breakdown.

Learning diagnosis per student
Beyond marks — VidyaNetra maps student answers to subject skill indicators (Equation Writing, Stoichiometry, Conceptual Understanding etc.) and flags which skills need attention vs are strong.
⚠️ "Needs attention" skills flagged per student
