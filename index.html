Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Does VidyaNetra replace teachers?
No. Teachers remain fully in control at every step — they review, adjust, and approve every evaluation before it is finalised. VidyaNetra handles the time-consuming parts so teachers can focus on what matters.
How accurate is the AI evaluation?
VidyaNetra achieves 80–85% accuracy on real student answer papers across subjects. Teachers review and correct the remaining cases in a fraction of the time full manual evaluation takes.
What subjects does it support?
All major school subjects — Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Social Studies, and Languages. VidyaNetra handles both theory answers and numerical or derivation-based questions.
How long does evaluation take?
A full class set of 40 answer papers is typically evaluated in under 10 minutes. The exact time depends on the number of questions and length of answers.
What format do answer sheets need to be in?
Scanned PDFs or images (JPG/PNG). Any standard school scanner or smartphone scan works. VidyaNetra handles handwritten scripts, printed answer books, and mixed formats.
Is student data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Student information is never shared with third parties and is used solely for evaluation within your school's account.
How do we start the free trial?
Simply fill in the contact form and we'll set everything up for you — one subject, one exam, one class section, completely free. No credit card, no commitment.
Does it work for board exam paper patterns?
Yes. VidyaNetra supports standard board exam formats including CBSE and state board patterns — across all mark weightages, question types, and subject structures.
